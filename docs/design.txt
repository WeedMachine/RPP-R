#### Simple Roleplay ###

--- Features ---
1) Minimal dialogs 
2) Easy to use controls
3) Fast gameplay

--- Gameplay Features ---
1) Mining with a pickaxe to get resources (f to start mining, t to cancel)
2) Inventory system which is based on size instead of weight.
3) Shops are visible objects.
4) Crafting instead of factories to make vehicles/items.
5) Tutorial camera - On creating a account you get shown around the map what you can do.
6) Police scanner

--- Admin Events ---
1) Quarantine - A virus spreads across the island and will spread to nearby people.

--- UI ---

Left side:

Money: Displays a picture of cash and next to it the amount of money you have.
